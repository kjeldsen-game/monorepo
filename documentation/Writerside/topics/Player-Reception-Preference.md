# Player Reception Preference

<primary-label ref="wip"/>

**Reception** is an attribute of players that defines the type of passes they prefer to receive.  
For example, players like **Messi** rarely receive high passes, while players like **Luca Toni** rarely receive low passes.

- **Applicable to:** All players, in offense, in the penalty box

---

## Types of Reception

### Demand High
**Effect:**
- 100% of the balls the player receives are **high passes**

### Demand Low
**Effect:**
- 100% of the balls the player receives are **low passes**

### Mixed
**Effect:**
- 50% of the balls the player receives are **high passes**
- 50% of the balls the player receives are **low passes**
